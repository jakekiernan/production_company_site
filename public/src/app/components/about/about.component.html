This is the part where we explain ourselves.

Who we are: a network of full-time filmmakers who share high standards and a joie de vivre.

What we’re working on: commercials, branded content, short films, and music videos.

Where we’re working: Alibi is based in New Orleans, Louisiana but our production contacts span the globe and we can be ready to travel at the drop of a hat.

We believe in process as experience with as much value as the final film — staying curious, being meticulous, using every resource to create something great. And sometimes that means staying up late and getting up early to work on content that communicates our clients’ core values with knowledge, innovation, skill, and style.

That’s pretty much it. That’s our alibi.
